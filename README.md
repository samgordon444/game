## Current Direction

**Concept:** Fantasy Exploration Simulator

**Core Concept:**
- **Genre:** Casual Exploration / Walking Simulator
- **Gameplay:** Side-scrolling exploration - aimless wandering through handcrafted fantasy landscapes
- **Character:** Relatable magical being (wizard or elf) - human-like, not too fantastical
- **Movement:** Walking primarily (side-scrolling), flying may come as discovery later
- **World:** Handcrafted, small to start (1-2 environments), expandable
- **Art Style:** Detailed pixel art - beautiful and polished
- **Collection:** Simple item gathering - no complex menus or nested structures
- **Playtime:** Flexible - enjoyable for 5 minutes or 5 hours
- **Experience:** Peaceful, aimless exploration with simple item collection - no quests, no pressure
